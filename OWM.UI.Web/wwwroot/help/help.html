FAQs

You can pledge mile by creating a team or joining a team.

Creating a team:


  • Mobile

    • log into your account.
    • Click on “Create a team” (by clicking on the hamburger menu(figure #01))
    • On the “Create a team” page, you should enter a name for your team
    • Number of miles you want to pledge as the team leader.
    • Description of you and your team.



  • Deskptop

    • log into your account.
    • Click on “Create a team” - you can find it on the side panel (figure 02)
    • On the “Create a team” page, you should enter a name for your team
    • Number of miles you want to pledge as the team leader
    • Description of you and your team

Join a team:

You can join a team in 2 different ways.

  • 1
    • log into your account.
    • Click on “my teams” link on the side menu under your profile picture.
    • Click on “Join a team” button.
    • Now you can scroll through teams and click on the team you want to join.
    • On the right top corner enter number of miles, you want to pledge and click on “pledge miles” button.
    • Now you are a member of that team.
  • 2
    • log into your account.
    • Click on “search teams” link on the side menu under your profile picture.
    • Now you can scroll through teams and click on the team you want to join.
    • On the right top corner enter number of miles you want to pledge and click on “pledge miles” button.
    • Now you are a member
